Common questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your Stripe billing portal at any time — no questions asked. You keep access until the end of the billing period.
What printers does it support?
Any printer that uses USB serial — Ender 3, Prusa MK3S/MK4, Voron, CR-10, Artillery, Anycubic Kobra, Neptune 4, Bambu Lab (serial mode), and any Marlin or Klipper printer with USB.
Does it work on Mac and Linux?
Yes. The desktop app is available for Windows 10/11, macOS, and Linux (.deb / .rpm). All platforms are included in a single subscription.
Is my payment secure?
Payments are handled entirely by Stripe — your card details never touch our servers. Stripe is used by millions of businesses worldwide.
What happens after I subscribe?
You're redirected back here instantly with access to the Windows, Mac, and Linux downloads. No email confirmation needed.
